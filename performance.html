Multi-Machine · RTX 4070 Super + Apple Silicon

Performance & Instrumentation

The system has evolved from a single-workstation pipeline to a multi-machine architecture: Windows handles ASR, TTS, and orchestration on the RTX 4070 Super; Apple Silicon hosts the LLM via MLX. The trade — modestly higher latency for substantially more capable inference — is documented below side-by-side with the original V1 numbers, end-to-end instrumented.

Stateful Chat — Sliding-Window Memory + Cross-Session Continuity

Voice and text conversations now maintain state. The last 10 turns of the active session are injected into prompt context. On disconnect, the session is summarized by Mac MLX and that summary becomes available to the next session. Per-persona separation is enforced at the schema level; conversation history is backed up to Supabase under a multi-tenant schema.
