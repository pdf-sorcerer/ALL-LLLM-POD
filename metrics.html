Realtime Voice Inference

Performance

The system is optimized to feel responsive during live conversation, not just score well on raw throughput benchmarks. Runtime tuning focused on reducing prompt orchestration overhead, minimizing blocking operations, and starting F5-TTS synthesis as early as possible during streaming generation. Different runtime configurations — including reasoning-enabled inference, richer system prompts, and alternative TTS models like VibeVoice — trade latency for response quality, personality fidelity, and conversational realism.
