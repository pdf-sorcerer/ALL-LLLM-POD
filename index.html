Multi-Agent LLM System

ALL-LLM Pod

Hey, everybody — welcome to the number one ALL-LLM Pod: a technically serious generative AI system built around an intentionally unserious premise: conversational chatbot parodies of the All-In Podcast hosts.

The personalities are reconstructed from hundreds of podcast episodes using an open-source CUDA pipeline for ASR, transcription, diarization, speaker classification, and transcript repair. The resulting datasets feed persona-specific orchestration and fine-tuning workflows designed to preserve speaker identity, tone, and conversational behavior.

I’m unsure where the line between parody and impersonation falls, but I wager — bravely — that since comedy is legal again, tech leaders who publicly admit not knowing which iPhone they use should probably expect at least a little teasing from people who do.
